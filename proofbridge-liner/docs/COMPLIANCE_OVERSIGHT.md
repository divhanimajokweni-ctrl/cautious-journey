# Compliance Oversight - ProofBridge Liner

## Regulatory Framework
- **Act 47**: Deeds registration integrity.
- **JS2**: Cyber incident reporting to FSCA.
- **POPIA**: Personal information protection.
- **Cybercrimes Act**: Forensic evidence collection.
- **FICA**: Suspicious activity reporting.

## Audit Process
- Hardware-signed logs in JSONL format.
- Monthly audit files with PII sanitization.
- Automated report generation for incidents.

## Certification
Designed for compliance with South African financial regulations.
