## Communication Guidelines

### Initial Outreach
- **Personalization**: Reference specific work relevant to recipient
- **Conciseness**: Keep emails under 300 words
- **Clear Ask**: Specify desired feedback areas
- **Easy Next Steps**: Provide direct links and clear instructions

### Follow-up Protocol
- **Timely**: Respond within 24 hours of any contact
- **Substantive**: Address technical points thoroughly
- **Appreciative**: Thank reviewers for their time and expertise
- **Documented**: Record all interactions and feedback

### Sensitive Handling
- **Anonymous Reviews**: Accept and properly handle anonymous feedback
- **Security Issues**: Have private channel for sensitive findings
- **Attribution**: Respect reviewer preferences on credit and visibility

## Contingency Planning

### Low Response Rate
- **Extend Timeline**: Continue outreach for additional week

### Technical Questions
- **Prepare Responses**: Have detailed answers ready for common questions
- **Escalation Path**: Route complex technical questions to appropriate team members
- **Documentation**: Update FAQ section based on common inquiries

### Security Concerns
- **Private Channel**: Maintain secure communication for sensitive findings

- **Documentation**: Record all security-related feedback appropriately
