Member questions

Who am I connected to?

Show member labels, facilitator, payout recipient, and verification state without exposing unnecessary private identity data.

02

Explain attendance, verified payment status, dispute history, and consent status before turning them into visual indicators.

03

What can the facilitator do?

Separate facilitator actions from member votes: identity review, reminders, queue management, and escalation logging.
