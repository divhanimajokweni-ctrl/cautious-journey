Audit model

Plain first, technical second.

Default view

Event name, amount, timestamp, affected member label, status, and next action.

Audit disclosure

Receipt ID, export, verifier status, and immutable proof details shown only when requested.

Privacy boundary

Use member labels or consented display names. Do not expose private identity documents in the group ledger.
