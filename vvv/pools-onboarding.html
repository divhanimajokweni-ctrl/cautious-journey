Application path

Every applicant should see the same decision path.

Eligibility
Applicant confirms age, location, WhatsApp access, and ability to meet the contribution schedule.
Step 1
Identity consent
Applicant sees what is collected, why it is collected, who can view it, and how deletion requests work.
Step 2
Pool rules
Contribution amount, due date, cycle length, payout order method, missed payment handling, and renewal rule.
Step 3
Governance agreement
Member accepts proposal, vote, dispute, facilitator, and veto-gate rules before entering the cohort.
Step 4
Application status
Pending, accepted, waitlisted, or declined with a plain-language reason and next action.
Manual pilot

Implementation guidance

Build this as a checkpoint flow, not a signup form.

Consent checkpoint

Use explicit checkboxes for identity verification, group visibility, contribution rules, and privacy policy acknowledgement.

No hidden obligations

Show amount, due dates, penalties, dispute route, and payout-order mechanics before collecting contact details.

Status visibility

Give applicants a status label and next action so manual facilitation does not feel like a black box.
